Our Mission

To empower students to become confident, compassionate, and responsible global citizens through holistic education, strong values, and lifelong learning.

Our Vision

We strive to create a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, creativity, discipline, and character-building—preparing students to face real-world challenges with skill and confidence.

The Aston Way:

Established with a vision to provide quality education and all-round development, Nachiketas high school and junior college is affiliated to State Board / CBSE and offers both day and residential schooling.

We combine strong academics with co-curricular and sports programs, ensuring every child grows intellectually, physically, and emotionally. Our fully-equipped hostel provides a secure, nurturing environment — a second home that supports learning beyond the classroom.
