Your Academic Path with Aston

SSC Board

Focused on building strong fundamentals and academic excellence under the Maharashtra State Board curriculum for Classes 1–10.

HSC Board

Comprehensive programs for Science and Commerce streams, with a focus on higher education and competitive exam preparation.

CBSE Board

We offers CBSE education focused on strong academics, concept clarity, and competitive exam readiness—backed by expert faculty and modern facilities.

NEET | JEE | MHT-CET

We provide in-school coaching for competitive exams like NEET, JEE, and MHT-CET, ensuring academic excellence and exam readiness.
